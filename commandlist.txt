LEFT - Move left by X amount.
RIGHT - Move right by X amount.

SCAN - Make the barcode scanner scan.
PHOTO - Take a photo with the camera.
ORIGIN - Move head to origin location.

USAGE:

LEFT <Value> , Returns: OK
RIGHT <Value> , Returns: OK

SCAN , Returns: OK <BARCODE_INFO> <SAVE_LOCATION>
PHOTO, Returns: OK <IMG_NAME> <IMG_LOCATION>
ORIGIN, Returns: OK
